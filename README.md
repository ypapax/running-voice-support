## Getting Started

### Setup
1. Grant **Location** permission (required for GPS tracking)
2. Grant **Motion & Fitness** permission (required for step counting)
3. Choose your preferred distance unit (kilometers or miles)
4. Toggle voice announcements on/off as desired

### During Your Run
- Press the green **Start** button to begin tracking
- Lock your phone to save battery‚Äîthe app continues tracking in the background
- First announcement at **10 meters** (30 feet)
- Regular announcements every **500 meters** (0.5 miles)
- Press **Stop** when finished to save your run

## FAQ

### Why do I need both Location and Motion permissions?
**Location** permission allows GPS tracking for accurate distance. **Motion & Fitness** permission enables step counting as a backup when GPS signal is weak. This dual approach ensures your run is always tracked reliably.

### Can I use the app without GPS?
Yes! The app uses step counting to track your distance when GPS is unavailable or unreliable.

### Does the app work with the screen locked?
Yes, the app continues tracking in the background. Lock your phone to save battery during your run.

### How do voice announcements work with music?
Voice announcements automatically lower your music volume, deliver the update, then restore your music volume. You don't need to pause anything.

### Can I turn off voice announcements?
Yes, go to **Settings** and toggle "Progress Announcements" off. You can still track your run without audio updates.

### Can I change units during a run?
No, distance units cannot be changed during an active run to maintain data consistency. Stop your run first, then change the unit in Settings.

### Where is my data stored?
All your run data is stored locally on your device. No account required, no cloud syncing, complete privacy.

## Troubleshooting

### GPS not working
- Check that Location permission is granted in **iPhone Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Location Services ‚Üí Running Voice**
- Enable "Always" or "While Using" permission
- Ensure you have a clear view of the sky (GPS works better outdoors)

### Steps not counting
- Check that Motion & Fitness permission is granted in **iPhone Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Motion & Fitness ‚Üí Running Voice**
- Restart your iPhone if the pedometer isn't responding

### No voice announcements
- Check that "Progress Announcements" is enabled in Settings
- Ensure your iPhone volume is turned up
- Check that Do Not Disturb mode is not blocking audio

### App crashes or freezes
- Force quit the app and reopen it
- Restart your iPhone
- Ensure you're running the latest version of iOS
